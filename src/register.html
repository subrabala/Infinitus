General rules

  • Registration is mandatory for Students to participate in INFINITUS 2023. Only registered students will be permitted to enter the college premises.
  • Registration Pass is valid only from 22nd to 24th April, 2023.
  • All participants are mandatorily required to bring a hard copy of their college Identity card and carry the INFINITUS band throughout the day.
  • No refund or cancellation policy: No monetary refunds will be given for any reason unless the fest gets canceled by the organizing committee.
  • Illegal participation of any participant in any event or competition will be responsible for disqualification of the participant of the concerned college from the event.
  • Any participating College/University can present an objection against the opponents of illegal participation or lack of adherence to rules, during a competition. Objections can be made either to the organizing committee or competition organizers.
  • Please do not carry/consume any intoxicants, narcotics, drugs, cigarettes or additives of any sort. Smoking is strictly prohibited within the campus.
  • Participants are required to present requested documents upon the demands of the organizing committee.
